About Online Quiz Application

Welcome to the Online Quiz Application! This platform provides users with a convenient way to take quizzes on various subjects. Whether you're a student looking to test your knowledge or an educator creating quizzes for your students, our application has you covered.

With our user-friendly interface, you can easily navigate through different quizzes, track your quiz history, and even create your own quizzes if you're an authorized user.

Start exploring our quizzes today and challenge yourself to learn something new!
