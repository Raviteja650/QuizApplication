About Quiz Application

Welcome to our online quiz application! Our platform aims to provide an interactive and engaging experience for users to test their knowledge in various subjects.

Features:

  • Take quizzes on a wide range of topics
  • Track your quiz history
  • Update your profile information
  • Logout securely

Technologies Used:

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, Bootstrap
  • Backend: Spring Boot, Thymeleaf
  • Database: MySQL

Feel free to explore our platform and enhance your knowledge through fun quizzes!
